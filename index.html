Work

Audiotool - (2024-Present)

My current job! I'm a backend engineer working on building the infrastructure for the redesign of a popular free online DAW that allows you to make songs through the browser and collaborate with friends.
VivaCity - (2021-2024)

Worked for a company building smart privacy-focused sensors that could record and classify traffic on the road through a local computer vision model. Initially worked on the Cloud team building customer facing dashboards, before becoming the team lead for Sensor Management with responsibility of managing/deploying the software side of the sensors and building internal tools for the company.
Metropolitan Police - (2020-2021)

Worked as a Digital Forensics Technician, mainly spent training before my previous development work got me transferred to a special project. Sadly, can't talk much about the work. That probably sounds exciting or secretive, but I assure you it was pretty mundane. An eye-opening experience, and taught me a lot.
The Indie Stone - (2016-2020)

My first job after university, I worked as a Gameplay Programmer for The Indie Stone working on Project Zomboid. Was a fan before I joined them, and it's crazy to think I contributed to a game played by so many. The joke I like to say when I meet fans of the game in person is: "Think of your favorite thing about the game. Have you done it? Good. I didn't work on that."
